About Us

Meet Sal Viglione

Viglione Investigations specializes in criminal defense investigations. If you have been a victim of an embarrassing arrest and are innocent, let us help you get the facts first to help support your case. Typically, most attorneys will not recommend a private criminal defense investigators because they do not want to share the cost or know very little about how a thorough investigation can really help win your case. We work with clients and their attorneys in both the private and the public defenders department. 

Before going to court, know the facts before you settle for a plea bargain for something you didn't do. Having a private investigator always helps your case, especially when it comes to sloppy police work and unmotivated attorneys.  

We have a high success rate in court reversing arrests that lack evidence and probable cause.

Let us help you! Call us now and don't fall victim to the system. Sal Viglione is a vendor for the CT Public Defenders Department. We handle cases where the State will pay for investigative services for clients who qualify. We have had success in high profile cases and work with attorneys who are knowledgeable and thorough who take a no nonsense and aggressive approach in court!

Sal Viglione is a retired police officer and current private investigator and have been working for attorneys since 2009. He has your best interest at heart and will help get you the justice you deserve!
